Process Update June 2026

New Autopay Enrollment Process — Billtrust

Clients are now enrolling in autopay through Billtrust instead of Client Connect. Client Connect autopay is being retired — any new enrollments must go through the Billtrust portal going forward.

If an office emails with autopay questions:
  • Clients currently enrolled in autopay through Client Connect do not need to re-enroll — their existing autopay will continue.
  • Any new autopay enrollments must be set up through the Billtrust portal at secure.billtrust.com/expresspros.
  • Direct the office to have the client log in (or register) at Billtrust, go to Account Settings, and select "Enroll in Autopay."
  • If the client needs help, the office can contact accounts-receivable@expresspros.com for assistance.

Technical Accounting & AR Knowledge

1

Understands basic AR functions (cash application, invoice processing). Follows step-by-step procedures with support.

2

Accurately performs all standard AR functions independently. Recognizes and resolves common discrepancies. Applies accounting principles to daily work.

3

Handles complex tasks, unapplied cash, and exceptions. Interprets financial data to identify patterns or process gaps. Provides expertise in policy or system usage.

Accuracy & Attention to Detail

1

Work occasionally requires correction; relies on supervisor for final review.

2

Work is consistently accurate with minimal review. Identifies and corrects minor inconsistencies proactively.

3

Consistently produces high-quality, accurate work. Reviews or supports peer work and helps identify root causes of recurring errors. Promotes best practices for accuracy.

Problem-Solving & Decision Making

1

Recognizes problems but seeks help for solutions.

2

Resolves moderately complex issues independently using established policies. Exercises judgment in unusual situations.

3

Independently resolves complex or non-routine issues. Anticipates potential problems and suggests practical solutions or process improvements. Serves as a resource to help others think through and resolve issues.

Customer Service & Communication

1

Responds to calls/emails courteously; may need guidance for complex inquiries.

2

Manages high-volume communication efficiently and professionally. Uses clear, concise, and respectful tone. Resolves most issues directly with franchise staff.

3

Handles complex or sensitive interactions with professionalism and composure. Provides guidance to others on communication approach. Consistently represents the team well in cross-functional interactions.

Independence & Initiative

1

Follows direction and established routines; asks for clarification as needed.

2

Works independently on assigned duties; identifies when to escalate issues.

3

Works autonomously on a wide range of responsibilities. Proactively identifies inefficiencies or gaps and takes initiative to suggest or implement improvements. Supports team progress without needing direction.

Teamwork & Leadership

1

Contributes to team goals; accepts feedback and direction.

2

Collaborates well; mentors new hires informally and shares knowledge. Demonstrates respect and professionalism in team interactions.

3

Acts as a strong team contributor and informal mentor. Provides guidance, support, and knowledge sharing to peers. Helps onboard or train others and contributes to a positive, professional team environment.

System & Technology Proficiency

1

Navigates core systems with supervision; limited reporting skills.

2

Uses multiple AR systems confidently; generates and interprets reports.

3

Serves as a go-to resource for system-related questions. Troubleshoots issues and supports others. Demonstrates advanced proficiency in Excel and other tools.

Confidentiality & Professionalism

1

Understands confidentiality expectations; adheres to policies. Maintains appropriate workplace behavior and communication.

2

Consistently maintains discretion with sensitive financial and employee information. Demonstrates professionalism in tone, behavior, and written communication.

3

Consistently models strong professional conduct. Maintains discretion in all situations and reinforces expectations through example. Helps guide peers in maintaining professionalism.

Professionalism, Reliability & Work Ethic

1

Meets basic attendance and punctuality expectations. Completes assigned tasks but may require reminders or follow-up. Demonstrates willingness to learn and accepts feedback.

2

Consistently reliable and dependable. Meets deadlines and manages time effectively. Communicates proactively regarding workload, delays, or challenges. Demonstrates accountability.

3

Highly reliable and consistent. Demonstrates strong ownership and follow-through. Proactively manages workload and supports team needs. Serves as a positive example of professionalism and work ethic for peers.
